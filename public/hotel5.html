Rambagh Palace, Jaipur, Rajasthan


Step into the regal world of Rajasthan at Rambagh Palace, a magnificent heritage hotel that seamlessly blends luxury with rich history.

Hotel Overview

Rambagh Palace is a stunning heritage hotel in Jaipur, once the residence of the Maharaja of Jaipur. Situated amidst lush gardens, the palace offers royal luxury and timeless elegance, making it an ideal destination for discerning travelers.

Amenities

  • Free High-Speed Wi-Fi
  • Spa and Wellness Center
  • Outdoor Pool & Jacuzzi
  • Fine Dining Restaurants
  • Fitness Center & Gym
  • Royal Concierge Service
  • Event & Conference Facilities

Location

Rambagh Palace is located at Bhawani Singh Road, Jaipur, Rajasthan 302005. It offers easy access to Jaipur's major attractions, including the City Palace, Jantar Mantar, and Hawa Mahal, all while maintaining an aura of tranquility.
