Taj Mahal Tower, Colaba, Mumbai


Experience luxury and history at the iconic Taj Mahal Tower, located in the heart of Colaba, Mumbai, offering breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea and Gateway of India.

Hotel Overview

Taj Mahal Tower, part of the Taj Group, is a landmark in Mumbai. This luxury hotel offers world-class amenities and service, providing guests with an unforgettable stay amidst Mumbai's vibrant culture and heritage.

Amenities

  • Free High-Speed Wi-Fi
  • Spa and Wellness Center
  • Outdoor Pool & Jacuzzi
  • Fine Dining Restaurants
  • Fitness Center & Gym
  • Exclusive Concierge Service
  • Event & Conference Facilities

Location

Located at Apollo Bunder Road, Colaba, Mumbai, the Taj Mahal Tower offers easy access to the Gateway of India, Elephanta Caves, and the vibrant Colaba Causeway for shopping and dining.
