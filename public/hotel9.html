Park Plaza, Ludhiana

The Park Plaza, Ludhiana, is licensed to operate as a Park Plaza Hotel by the Radisson Hotel Group. The hotel is managed by Sarovar Hotels.

Hotel Overview

Park Plaza, Ludhiana, is a premium hotel by Radisson Hotel Group and managed by Sarovar Hotels. This is a 5-star hotel in Ludhiana located just 10 minutes from Ludhiana railway station. The grand lobby and open terrace give a magical aura of the hotel. The hotel features spacious contemporary rooms and 4 dining options - a multi-cuisine restaurant, a lounge bar, a Chinese & Pan Asian restaurant, and a sports bar. Organise your social functions and business meetings at one of our 9 splendid banqueting venues. There is a range of facilities available in the hotel like a fitness centre, boxing ring, and a spa to keep you engaged, entertained, and rejuvenated during your stay at the hotel. Get assistance from our travel desk to plan your trip to Ludhiana. This is one of the best hotels in Ludhiana whether you are travelling for business or pleasure.
